About

Kore-G

Kore-G is an electronic music producer based in Melbourne, Australia. Known for his innovative approach, he merges elements of psytrance with bass music, among other genres to create a unique & psychedelic sonic space.

Extending beyond music production, he is deeply involved in art & technology, from self-made 3D animations to the mixing and mastering of his tracks to creating entire sample packs on Splice. Over the years, Kore-G has played at Australian festivals such as Esoteric, Rabbits Eat Lettuce, and Aum Festival in New Zealand.

His sample packs have been supported by worldwide including Sara Landry, Vini Vici, Reinier Zonneveld & Indira Paganotto.
