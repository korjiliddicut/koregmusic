Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kore-G ("we", "us", or "our") handles your personal information when you visit koregmusic.github.io (the "Site"), interact with our social media channels, or buy our products. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

We keep things deliberately simple. The Site itself is a basic, static website that collects very little information directly. Most of the personal information involved in a purchase is handled by Gumroad, the platform we use to sell and deliver our sample packs.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we do, we'll post the revised version here and update the "Last updated" date above.

Information we collect

Information you give us directly. If you contact us by email or through our social media accounts, we receive whatever information you choose to include, such as your name and email address.

Purchases. We don't process payments ourselves. When you buy a sample pack, the transaction is handled by Gumroad, which collects the information needed to take payment and deliver your download (such as your name, email address, and payment details). Gumroad handles this information under its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to read: Gumroad's Privacy Policy.

Usage information. Our Site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub may automatically record technical information such as your IP address and browser type when you visit, in order to serve the Site.

Cookies

Our Site does not set its own tracking or advertising cookies. Some third-party services we link to or embed — such as Gumroad, or social platforms like Instagram, YouTube and TikTok — may set their own cookies if you interact with them; those are governed by the relevant third party's policy. You can control or block cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as well if you do.

How we use your information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • respond to you and provide customer support;
  • fulfil and support your orders (together with Gumroad);
  • send you updates or marketing only where you've signed up to receive them — you can opt out at any time;
  • protect against fraud or misuse; and
  • comply with our legal obligations.

How we share your information

We don't sell your personal information. We share it only where necessary, for example:

  • with Gumroad, to process payments and deliver your purchases;
  • with service providers who help us operate the Site;
  • where required to comply with the law or to protect our rights.

Third-party sites and links

Our Site links to other platforms, including Gumroad and our social media profiles. We aren't responsible for the privacy practices of those sites, so we encourage you to review their policies before sharing information with them.

Security and retention

No method of transmitting or storing data is perfectly secure, so we can't guarantee absolute security; please avoid sending sensitive information through insecure channels. We keep personal information only for as long as we need it to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete it, or to opt out of marketing. To make a request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before we respond.

International users

We're based in Australia, and the services we use (such as Gumroad and GitHub) may store and process information in other countries, including the United States. By using the Site, you understand that your information may be processed outside your home country.

Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your information, please contact us first. If you're in Australia and aren't satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC). If you're elsewhere, you may be able to contact your local data protection authority.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at djkoreg@gmail.com.
